This My Vocabulary Webpage For Week 10

meta tag for charset

a HTML tag that contains metadata about a web page, or more specifically, descriptors that tell search engines what type of content a web page contains that is hidden from display

meta tag for keywords

meta tags that you can use to give search engines more information about a page's content

meta tag for viewport

that tells browsers how to control viewport dimensions and scaling

meta tag for refresh

a method of instructing a web browser to automatically refresh the current web page or frame after a given time interval,

meta tag for author

that tells search engines and readers who wrote a web page

Confidentiality (cybersecurity) part of AIC triad

the efforts of an organization to make sure data is kept secret or private

Integrity (cybersecurity) part of AIC triad

data are trustworthy, complete, and have not been accidentally altered or modified by an unauthorized user

Availability (cybersecurity) part of AIC triad

systems, networks, and applications must be functioning as they should and when they should

White Hat Hacker (cybersecurity)

an ethical security hacker. Ethical hacking is a term meant to imply a broader category than just penetration testing. Under the owner's consent

black hat hacker (cybersecurity)

a computer hacker who violates laws or ethical standards for nefarious purposes, such as cybercrime, cyberwarfare, or malice. These acts can range from piracy to identity theft

Requirements Traceability

a sub-discipline of requirements management within software development and systems engineering.

Iron Triangle (project management)

he Iron Triangle refers to the three key constraints that can affect a project. These are budget, scope and schedule
